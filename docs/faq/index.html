FAQ

Video

Q. Do you think I can practice playing music with an app that only plays videos?
Here are some things you can do to improve your practice.
- No pausing by tapping the screen during playback
- You can set the playback area
- You can adjust the playback speed
- Video expansion, contraction and movement is possible

Just tapping the screen while playing the part you're weak at is good enough practice üí™
So start practicing today ‚ô™~

Q. Can I orient it vertically?
"Video Selection" You can change the orientation by tapping the "Device is rotating" icon in the lower left corner of the screen.
Tap again to return to landscape mode.

Q. Is it possible to make a touch sound?
You can set up the touch sound in "Settings > Settings on Video > Sound" in the app.
There are different types of touch sounds, so you can choose the one you like best ‚ô™~

Q. Can I see the touch points?
You can adjust the display settings for touch from "Settings > Settings on Video > Point" in the application.
You can also customize the color and size of the display to make it your own. ‚ô™~

Q. Can I adjust the volume of the touch tone?
You cannot do this in the app. Please adjust the volume on your device.

Q. The home bar gets in the way during pseudo-play, making it difficult to play. Is there a home bar setting?
You can ignore the first Home Bar operation by going to "Settings > Video > Home Bar" in the application.

Q. I'm having trouble with the tap position and the pause button being in the same place in some playback videos. Can you fix this?
You can change the pause button to a long tap.
On the "Folder List" screen, you can select "Edit > Settings on Video > Pause button" to switch to long tap.

Q. Where can I set up the touchscreen display in the application?
On the "Folder List" screen, you can set it up by going to "Edit > Select Folder > App Type".

Q. Can you fix the sound shift?
There is no way to solve this problem within the application, but I hope to address it someday.

Q. Is there a way to fast-forward or rewind a few seconds?
You can set it using the "Folder Settings > Fast forward/rewind button" button at the bottom left of the folder/video list screen.
You can also adjust the position of the display on the video screen and the number of seconds ‚ô™~

Q. Can I reposition or hide the rewind/fast forward button?
You can change or hide the position.
You can set it using the "Folder Settings > Fast forward/rewind button" button at the bottom left of the folder/video list screen.

Q. The resolution of my YouTube videos is low. Can I change it?
You can choose from 240p/360p/720p. The default setting is 360p.
You can change the YouTube quality from the gear icon in the bottom left of the video screen.

Q. When will the "xxx" application type be added?
It is difficult to support the recent iOS devices due to the increase in the number of different ratios. In addition, there are more and more apps that allow you to adjust the touchline position on the sound game app side, so it is not worth it.
This is why the frequency of updating the app types is low. However, that doesn't mean we don't want to increase the frequency.

Requests/bugs

Q. Where do I submit my request?
Please send us your request from "Contact" in the app. We can't accept all requests, but we will consider them.
I'm also open to reviews on the AppStore. However, it is difficult to communicate with them, so we may not be able to understand their intentions.

Q. Where do I report bugs?
Please send us your request from the "Contact" section of the app.
Since it is difficult to communicate with AppStore reviews, it may not be possible to reproduce them and there may be a delay in responding.

Q. YouTube videos no longer play in the app. Is this a bug?
It's most likely a bug. Please contact us from the "Contact" section of the app, including the URL of the video that is not playing.

Q. When I use the fast forward/rewind buttons or play a video, it pauses. Is this a bug?
For YouTube videos, the resolution may be too high for smooth playback. You can change the YouTube quality from the gear icon in the bottom left of the video screen.
For other videos, the resolution of the original video file may be too high. Please re-import the video at a lower resolution.

Q. Videos from my camera roll no longer play in the app. Is this a bug?
Deleting a video from the camera roll will stop it from playing.
If you choose to save photos to iCloud, they will not be playable at a later date. Please register the video uploaded to iCloud to the application.
If you have videos in your camera roll but they do not play, try restarting the app/device to see if that solves the problem.

Q. The video position appears in a strange place on the video screen. Can this be fixed?
After clicking "Play > Stop" on the video screen, touch the "grid icon" in the lower left corner of the screen and swipe the screen to move the position.
